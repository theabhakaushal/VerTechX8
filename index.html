ABOUT THE EVENT

VERTECHX 8.0

VerTechX 8.0 brings to you the most daunting and challenging technical fest of 2018, hosted by MVJ College of Engineering. On the 2nd and 3rd of April 2018, the nation will witness a technical revolution lead by the youth with a persistent goal in mind. Technology, with its new age innovations, has been nothing less than pure magic, and we aim to offer the best minds of the nation an opportunity to portray their talents.

The fest will host a crowd like never before, from the mind-trotters battling it out with their bots at the 'Fighter Bot' event, all the way to the sapient bunch waging a war at the 'ROBO-SOCCER' event. With prizes worth INR 500000, there is only one question you must ask yourself. "Is my brain wired differently?"
